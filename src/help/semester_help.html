Getting Started
To get to the semester page you can click on the Semester icon on the home page. On the semester page you can add a new semester, edit semester year and/or term or delete only if the course count is zero.
Add Semester

(1) Start of by clicking on "Select Term". You should see a drop-down list to select from.

(2) From the drop-down list, select a semester e.g. Winter 2020.

(3) In the year field enter the semester year. A valid year range will be displayed in the year field e.g. 2000 to 2020.

(4) Once the year term and year has being filled out go ahead and click the add button. You should then see a Success! dialogue box.

(5) In the Success dialogue box click on ok. To confirm your semester has been successfully added you can view it in the existing semester list.

View Semester

(1) To view an added semester click on the semester year in the existing semester list e.g. 2020. You will see a drop-down with the list of semester for the given year (Semester years are listed in descending order).

(2) In the drop list for the existing semester year you can edit a semester term and/or yea, delete a semester with course count zero and view course count for each semester.

Edit Semester

(1) Start of by clicking on the semester year containing the semester you want to edit e.g. 2020. You should see a drop-down with the list of semesters.

(2) In the drop-down list of semesters click on the edit button besides the semester you want to edit. This should change the add box to the edit box above the exiting semesters.

(3) In the Edit Semester box, you can update the term and/or the year of the semester. In our case we will update Winter 2020 to Fall 2019.

(4) After updating the semester click update to begin saving change(s) made or cancel to discard any change(s) made. After clicking update you will see a popup dialogue box.

(5) In the pop-up dialogue box click yes to give permission to continue making changes or no to cancel and continue editing.

(6) After clicking yes you should then see a Success! dialogue box once the semester has being updated along with all other files associating with the semester. Click ok and you are finish. To confirm the semester was successfully updated you can view it in the existing list of semesters.

Delete Semester

(1) Start of by clicking on the semester year containing the semester you want to delete e.g. 2020. You should see a drop-down with the list of the semester(s). Only semester with a course count of Zero can be deleted.

(2) In the drop-down list for the semester year, click on the delete button besides the semester you want to delete. After clicking update you will see a popup dialogue box.

(3) In the pop-up dialogue box click yes to give permission to continue deleting semester or no to cancel.

(4) After clicking yes to delete the semester, "Semester deleted successfully" will be displayed in the top right for a few seconds.

(5) You can confirm a semester was successfully removed by viewing the list of remaining semesters for the year if it is the only semester the year will be removed from the existing semester list.
