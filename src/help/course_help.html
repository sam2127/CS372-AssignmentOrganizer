ADD COURSE

(1) Search for the semester you want to add a course for e.g. Winter 2020. You Can select from the search result in the drop down menu.

(2) Type your course code.

(3) Type your course name.

(4) Click add.

(5) Click on the year to view the list of Course(s)

EDIT COURSE

(1) Click on the year containing the course you want to modify.

(2) Next to the course you want to modify, click the edit button.

(3) In the box, you can modify the semester, course code and/or course name.

(4) Click update to save change(s) or cancel if you do not want to make change(s)

DELETE COURSE

(1) Click on the year containing the course you want to delete.

(2) Next to the course you want to delete, click the delete button.

(3) From the pop-up dialog box select Yes to confirm deleting the course or No to cancel deleting the course.
