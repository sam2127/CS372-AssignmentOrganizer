Introduction

Students experience a barrage of assignments, projects, reports, etc. over the lifetime of their course. They may acquire a substantial digital load by the end of their first year and need help to sort it out. This is where our app, Assignment Organizer serves its purpose. It is a multi-platform app designed to help users systematically organize their assignments by year/term and course.

Getting Around

(1) On the main page of the application, you will find 4 button that leads to the 4 main pages. There are some basic instructions to follow already on the home page to get you started.

(2) The Back-Up Settings button takes you to the settings page. Here you can choose where the app will store your files by default.

(3) We have included counters that allow you to see at a glance how many semester, courses and assignments you have added.

(4) The menu at the top allows you to get to a from pages with ease.

(5) The go menu button provides quick links to the five main pages in the application.

(6) The help menu button provides the options help, which is the manual/guide for the application, and about which gives contains the credits and other auxiliary information.
